About | Science & Technology Council

Welcome to the homepage of the Science and Technology Council, IIT (BHU) Varanasi! This is the place where all your innovative ideas, which were trapped in the quagmire of pen and paper, are given shape and transformed into reality. Our council houses a plethora of clubs, which are extremely adept at their job. From making robots that battle it out amongst themselves in an arena to making racing cars, we do it all.
