About

At FAC, we are involved in sketching, painting, calligraphy, cartooning, paper-cutting, sculpting, quilling, 3-D street painting and other cool stuff. We organize events like IIT-Open (internal event of college) and Toolika (during Kashi Yatra) where competitions like Art Marathon, Face-Painting, Card-Making, Sketching etc. are conducted. Apart from the above, we also work together with the teams of KY, Technex and Spardha for the decoration of the college during the fests. FAC has often been adjudged as the best club of the cultural council and has won prizes at cultural fests of other IITs as well.
